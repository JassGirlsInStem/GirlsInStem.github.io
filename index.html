WHO WE ARE

The JASS Creative Council is the hub of creativity at Jean Augustine Secondary School. Whether its visual arts, digital tech, analog tech, and even performance arts, we are here to create and inspire. Our overall goal is to build a more lively environment for our school and to inspire others to use their imagination above and beyond!
