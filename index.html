About Us!

In 2019, Girls in STEM was founded at Jean Augustine Secondary School with the mission to empower young women to join academic fields. While STEM is an expanding field, women make up a smaller percentage of workers compared to men, resulting in a significant gender imbalance. Recognizing the problem laid the groundwork for the rest of our efforts. Our organization's principal goal, in the form of various initiatives, became translating discussion and theory of these social injustices into tangible action and impassioned advocacy.
